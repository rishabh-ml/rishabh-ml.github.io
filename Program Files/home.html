About Us

Syed Rizvi Technical Services L.L.C, established in 2023, is a leading provider of maintenance and technical services across the UAE. We specialize in building maintenance, plumbing, AC repair, cleaning services, and more, catering to residential and commercial sectors.

Our mission is to deliver quality services tailored to the needs of our clients using state-of-the-art equipment and eco-friendly products. We take pride in our commitment to integrity, safety, and client satisfaction, offering innovative solutions to ensure the longevity and performance of your assets.

Our core values revolve around teamwork, leadership, and fostering strong relationships with clients, ensuring their goals and expectations are met effectively.
