About Us

Your Trusted Partner for Building Maintenance and Technical Services

Who We Are

Syed Rizvi Technical Services L.L.C was established in 2023 with the aim to provide exclusive maintenance and technical services to our valuable clients, with specific prominence on quality and safety. With our 360-degree adaptability in assorted services, building and maintenance skills, reasonable pricing, and enhanced working exposure, services provided by our experienced team are highly appreciated across the UAE.

Our Vision

As a full-service building maintenance and cleaning company, our customer's satisfaction is our number one priority. We provide the option of non-toxic products and eco-friendly equipment to ensure a clean, healthy, and safe environment for all of our clients. In the scope of conveniences maintenance, our clients can depend on us to provide quality cleaning services, plumbing services, professional operations and maintenance, integrated facility management, and strong electrical work.

Building Maintenance of Today is a single-source vendor of exterior and interior commercial cleaning services and facility maintenance services for commercial buildings. We specialize in providing professional building maintenance services to residential and commercial sectors like flats, villas, banks, retail, schools, hospitals, and commercial buildings.

Our Mission

With our flexible janitorial service plans tailored to meet each individual client's specific needs and budget, we have been able to continually simplify office cleaning and janitorial services. We are dedicated and committed to a higher standard of service and a healthier environment. At Syed Rizvi Technical Services L.L.C, we pride ourselves on our superior office cleaning techniques, customer service, and quality assurance programs.

The main agenda of Syed Rizvi Technical Services L.L.C is to provide ample maintenance and cleaning services to all clients, using the latest equipment and technology.
