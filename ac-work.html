Common Air Conditioning Problems We Fix

Our technicians are skilled in diagnosing and repairing a wide range of AC issues, including:

  • The air conditioner won't turn on: This could be due to electrical issues, thermostat malfunctions, or a tripped circuit breaker.
  • Warm air blowing from vents: Potential causes include low refrigerant levels, compressor problems, or a faulty thermostat.
  • Strange noises or vibrations: Unusual sounds may indicate loose components, motor issues, or debris in the system.
  • Fan or compressor not working: Motor failures or electrical issues can prevent these essential components from operating.
  • Water pooling around the unit: This could be a sign of a clogged drain line, frozen evaporator coils, or refrigerant leaks.
